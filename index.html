About Us

Super Subs is a place where customers become regulars and regulars become friends. We are your home away from home, the place where you’ll always get more and more. We’ve gone the extra mile to bring you all your familiar favourites as well as the latest sandwich innovations. Each sandwich is generously stacked with fresh ingredients of the highest quality so that you can enjoy more of that nutritious taste. We’re serious about sandwiches and we take care to hand select the best ingredients from the regions all over the world. Then we process and prepare them locally before our professionally trained sandwich makers make the perfect submarine sandwiches, just for you.
